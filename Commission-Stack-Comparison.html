The bottom line for partners

  • The whole decision is a Comfort / Comfort+ closer question. Setter ($1,000), manager override ($1,000) and the Premium closer ($5,000) are fixed, so Premium returns $8,317 at 29.7% no matter which option we pick.
  • From Lean to Generous we give back at most $1,000 of margin on any single deal, and only on Comfort and Comfort+. The most exposed deal — a Comfort sale on the Generous stack — still nets $4,383 (23.7%).
  • We keep more than half of every gross dollar on every deal, in every option (the navy slice never drops below ~47%). The richer the stack, the bigger the closer's slice — exactly where extra comp can lift conversion on the lower tiers.
  • Self-gen vs team deals don't change our cost: a self-gen rep keeps Setter + Closer; on a team deal that $1,000 splits off to the setter. The commission stack — and every margin above — is the same either way.
Modeled at California base cost with a 0% cost cut — drop the cost and every margin here only climbs. Commission stack = Setter + Closer + Manager override. Net profit = gross profit − commission stack. Margin = net ÷ selling price. Avg margin = simple average of the three tier margins.
